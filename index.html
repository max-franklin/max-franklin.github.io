Hi, I'm Max

Who I Am

I'm an Alaskan, born and raised in Anchorage. I have a love for the outdoors that only comes with growing up in it. I understand the unique perspective that is granted when one has the opportunity to disconnect from society and enjoy nature. I think people are at their best when they're able to escape and get away from the bustle of society from time to time. I understand that sometimes, you just need to get away from city living and reset so that when you get back to work on Monday, you can bring your best game. You can find elements of my Alaskan background both in my work and in my hobbies.

What I Do

I'm a programmer. I have an unorthodox educational background for software development, with a B.S. in Biological Sciences and a M.S. in Civil Engineering. It is precisely this background that enables my quick and efficient performance as a programmer; I understand that software development is about products that work, and I know how to deliver them agilely. My self-taught programming background means I know how to quickly learn new languages outside the educational framework of a classroom. I'm not afraid to dive into documentation to solve problems and optimize my code. My previous work has centered around development of agent-based models and scripting for volumentric analysis at the Anchorage Landfill. Head on over to my work page to read more about the projects I've been involved with.

My Passions and Interests

When I'm not working, I'm usually learning a new programming language or working on personal coding projects. Coding aside, I love getting outdoors, playing my piano, or getting deep into a science fiction or fantasy novel.
